Opening Celebration of LÓKAL 2019

8 pm - Tjarnarbíó

Honorable assembly, dear guests. Dear friends and family. Honorable guests, friends and family, moderator. Honourable moderator, companions, ladies and gentlemen. Beloved friends. Beloved family and gathering, gathily, famering, fatherly, gamering, assembly, president and representatives. The show is about to begin. Please take your seals


Artist Snæfríður Sól Gunnarsdóttir will open the festival with a ritual. Snæfríður recently graduated from the Theatre and Performance Making department at the Iceland University of the Arts. Besides that, she is also educated in dance and fine arts and likes to melt all the disciplines together.


Three Inspireres will give artist talks on their creative process. The Inspires are: Eirún Sigurðardóttir - fine artist/performance artist, Guðrún Eva Mínervudóttir - writer and Margrét Bjarnadóttir dancer/performance artist.

New artistic directors will go over the program shortly. Skál and the bar is open.

Free entrance to all this years LÓKAL performances!
