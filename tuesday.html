Crash Course in constructive feedback

12 pm - Tjarnarbíó

A crash course in constructive feedback. Learn some basic do´s and don´ts from Ólöf Ingólfsdóttir who has studied feedback and helped artist in Reykjavík for years now.

Ólöf is a dancer and a performance artist. She is a project manager at Dansverkstæðið - work rooms for choreographers. She has studied the constructive feedback methods by Liz Lerman.

Free entrance to all this years LÓKAL performances!
