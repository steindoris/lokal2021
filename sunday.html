Open showcase from Kveikjur
Works in progress from the work shop will be shown

14 pm - Tjarnarbíó

Artists participating in Kveikjur:


Andrea Vilhjálmsdóttir with a seed sprung from a box of her own children’s clothing, forgotten but kept in the attic for the past 25 years.

Andrea Elín Vilhjálmsdóttir is a performance and theater artist from Reykjavík. During the past years she has directed, been a dramaturge and performed in various productions. To mention a few, Velkomin heim (e. Welcome home) and Hún Pabbi (e. She Dad) with Trigger Warning.


Ragnar Ísleifur Bragason with a seed sprung from a hotel-lobby manager working night shifts and listening repeatedly to a song by an Icelandic pop band with epic lyrics, centering around the epochs of every man's life.

Ragnar Ísleifur Bragason is a stage artist amongst other things. He enjoys all this.


Ragnheiður Harpa Leifsdóttir with a seed sprung from the attraction of theatre seen from the people working behind the scenes.

Ragnheiður Harpa Leifsdóttir’s multidisciplinary practice spans the fields of performance, visual arts and writing.


Tatjana Dís with a seed sprung from dreams and all that is uncomprehensible

Tatjana is a performance artist. Her work is visual and atmospheric. She takes narratives, archetypes and clichés from movies, and works with them on stage. As an Artist she is drawn to what is familiar but at the same time alienated.
