National History of Queuing

8 pm - Tjarnarbíó

DíÓ (Aðalbjörg Árnadóttir and Ylfa Áskelsdóttir)

A conversation on queues, Icelanders, greed and what it is to be a nation among nations.

Aðalbjörg and Ylfa are Reykjavík based actressess. They are both members of the performance group 16 lovers. DÍÓ has brought us: It´s Volleyball Hallelujah. Now they investigate the history of queues.

Music at the bar

After the show

Björg Elínar - Sveinbjörnsdóttir presents us with an electronic musical in three parts, based on the Red book Series, June edition.

Björg is an artist, teacher and a founder of the Museum of Everyday life in Ísafjörður.
